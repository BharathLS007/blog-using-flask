An Adventurous World


“Oh Havana na na na, my heart is in Havana na na na…”


Yep, like everyone else I left a little bit of my heart in Havana.

With its rustic charm of old colourful colonial buildings to the kind-hearted

people you meet on the streets to its rich and vibrant history, Havana has a soul

to it rarely found anywhere else in the world.


Even though the far majority of people just visit Havana, the rest of the country

is incredible too with so much to see and do.


There are beautiful beaches to laze, forests and coffee plantations to discover,

mountains with caves to explore.

From rural Viñales to urban Havana, there really is something for everyone here

and you’ll love it.
